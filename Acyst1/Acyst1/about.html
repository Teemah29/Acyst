About

ACYST TECHNOLOGY CO. LTD. was founded in 2016 by Stephen Osei, with the believe that entrepreneurship through technology is what will move our beloved Africa into the international limelight. The company is full of millennials with tremendous skills and passion to make this dream a reality.

The company lso exhibits a great company culture by way of our attitude towards the work, our relationship with each other and sense of purpose towards the task ahead. We aim to create an environment any millennial would want to work to become a better version of themselves, because what wakes us up in the morning is not the money and all the benefits, although necessary, but it is the "WHY" we do what we do. Not only are we a 'MILLENNIAL' Company, we are also a multi-national company that pride itself with Executives from three African Nations -- Ghana, Nigeria, and Chad.

Our Mission

"To become an iconic technology company that seeks to make a difference through inventions and renovations, by delivering safe industrial and human solutions in all openness and integrity."

Our Vision

"To establish itself as Africa's leading giant in the tech industry: By coming up with not just innovative, but disruptive products."
